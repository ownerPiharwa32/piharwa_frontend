ABOUT US

OUR VISION - Our Vision is to combine style with comfort and trends with tradition in your everyday styling. We take pride in bringing best of the handlooms of India in the form of sarees, lehangas and poshak as well as beautiful and trendy Indo-western collection. Piharwa strives to enhance your style statement with all natural fibre fabric and celebrate womanhood by seeking perfection in imperfection of handloom

OUR MISION - Inspired by the rich and varied heritage of India, our mission is to bring to you the real art of India by the real artisans. We strive to encourage the beauty and uniqueness of the skillful hands which is lost with the uniformity and perfection of machines. We take pride to be associated with the real artisans and to promote them thereby doing our bit in preserving the form of art of rich and royal heritage of India
